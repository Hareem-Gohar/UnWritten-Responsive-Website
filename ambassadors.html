Paula Hibbard

Freelance Hairdresser & Independent Hairstylist in Sydney

Welcome to the world of long hair styling and the magical creations of Paula Hibbard, an exceptional freelance hairdresser and independent hairstylist based in the vibrant city of Sydney. With a passion for sharing knowledge and empowering fellow hairdressers, makeup artists, and salon groups, Paula's customised training programs are second to none. Let's dive into her journey, accomplishments, and three intriguing facts that make her a standout in the hairdressing industry.

A Trailblazing Hairdressing Journey

Paula Hibbard's story began on the Sunshine Coast of Queensland, where she embarked on her hairdressing career as a young teenager with the renowned Jon Le Court hairdressing group. Over the years, she climbed the ladder of success, transitioning from a talented stylist to a skilled salon manager and eventually becoming an area manager for the group in New South Wales. Throughout her journey, Paula explored various roles, including sales and management, but her true passion was ignited during her tenure as a colour technician for the product giant, Goldwell. It was here that she discovered her love for education and inspiring others.
With an insatiable appetite for knowledge, Paula's talent caught the attention of a leading Australian and International long hair artist and salon owner. This partnership lasted an impressive 15 years, during which Paula traveled the world as a head teacher, delivering classes and bootcamps to hairdressers and salons across the globe. Her dedication and expertise eventually led her to launch her own successful full-time training company, Paula Hibbard Education, in 2014. Today, she spends her days traveling around Australia and internationally, providing bespoke long hair training programs for salons and individuals.

A Passionate Hairdressing Maven

Globetrotting Long Hair Artist

Paula's creative flair and technical expertise have taken her to the forefront of the global hairdressing scene. Her work has been featured in prestigious magazines like Modern Hair & Beauty, Oyster, InStyle, and Modern Wedding, both in Australia and overseas. Beyond print media, Paula's skills have graced the screens of film, fashion, and television productions, including Project Runway, The Voice Kids, Mercedes Benz Australian Fashion Week, and The Gods of Egypt.

A Heart for Brides

While Paula's talents span various hairdressing domains, her true passion lies in creating stunning upstyles for brides on their special day. Her genuine love for the art of bridal hairstyling shines through in every intricate detail she crafts, making her a sought-after choice among brides looking for that perfect touch of elegance and charm.

Beyond the Hairdressing World

When Paula is not busy transforming locks into works of art, she enjoys cherishing life's simple pleasures. As a dedicated soccer mom, she supports her two sons on the field, and her love for exploring new horizons takes her on exciting travels through Europe. Additionally, Paula relishes her downtime with a cup of coffee, indulging in delectable culinary delights, and enjoying the effervescence of bubbles.
